Bushfires in Australia

Bushfires is happening every year around Australia in different places. It causes to kill the native animals and people as well as some bush. Australia wild wide animals and plants are developed in the movement of bushfires. Indigenous people and environment accept this movement, but each bushfire is getting worth every time due climate change and unnecessary deforestation. A large bushfire can give a multiple impact to different industry. For example, decreasing of the rural population, smoke, water resources, environmental issues for the wild animals, and increase of government budget, insurance and other recovery costs.

There are several possible reasons for the devastating fires in Australia. Firstly, and mainly weather condition on that year effect the size of bushfire especially little rain and dry. Australia is known as one of the dry places in the world. Country needs some rainfall through the year to keep away from the bushfires. Also, high temperature helped to fire. Secondly, trees that natively grown in Australia. Some of them especially eucalyptus produces highly volatile oils in its leaves and releases gases containing them into the air. Thirdly is caused by the human. Deforestation or arson become very popular factor to be fire on summer time.

Overviews of 2019-2020's Bushfire

On late 2019, Queensland fire emergency warned the potential bush fire towards the end of year. On 28th of October, estimate 14.3 million hectares are burnt and over 3000 buildings are destroyed. End of March 2020, around 30,000,000 hectors are burned and approximately 9300 buildings are destroyed also 24 people died entire bushfire event. Fires are on entire of country especially east coast of QLD, NSW and Northern Territory.

In this Bushfire, fire produce lot of serious smoke around southern hemisphere. Australia major cities were also recorded serious smoke and some citizens are felt dyspnea. New Zealand, Brazil and south Africa record smoke what flew and covered south pacific and Indian ocean. The Photo shows the DC10 fire tanker from US.

As reactions from outside of Australia, there was also some overseas help. Few countries army and worldwide bushfire emergency service came to Australia to help people as well as firefighting. Some world competitions had force to cancel their competitions due fire. Finally on March of 2020, NSW declare the ends of nations Bushfires.

Impacts of 2019-2020's Bushfire

There was the huge impact to the wild animals around Australia. The study, conducted by scientists from multiple universities, found wildlife including 143 million mammals, 2.46 billion reptiles, 180 million birds and 51 million frogs. was damaged. Also, The prospects for the animals that survived the fires "would not be very good," as they lost food and habitat and were unable to protect themselves from predators, Chris Dickman said on his interview. Australian official said, "It's one of the worst wildlife disasters in modern history."

The situation of the koalas are even worth than other animals. Nearly 8000 koalas have been killed due this bush fires. In Kangaroo Island in south Australia, more than half of habitat were burned and 80% of koalas have lost their home. 650 koalas had rescued, and island is now recovering to the new period.

However, there is some problem for the recovery and future. Bushfire is often happened in Australia, but 2019-2020 bushfires were very massive and abrupt changes in very short term. This change may burn all seed or any other nutrition or the recovery. Also, animals lost their home. Previously, changes are not very quick, animals were able to find another place to stay to be away from fire and other natural enemy. But these days bushfire are not able to react the fire because all movement is very quick. Abrupt change is not the grate think in environment. This is the time for the world citizen to think about the climate change and earth environment to make the calm and peaceful earth.
